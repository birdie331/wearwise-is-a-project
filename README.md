# WearWise is a Project

## Project Overview
A custom application that WearWise is a smart, AI-powered outfit-planning app designed to make getting dressed faster, easier, and more personalized while helping users get the most out of the clothes they already own. At its core, the app digitizes your closet: users upload photos of their clothing or scan items with the “Describe My Closet” feature (powered by a vision API). WearWise automatically organizes each item by type, color, and category, creating a fully searchable digital wardrobe.

From there, the AI generates tailored outfit suggestions based on multiple factors: the occasion (school, casual, formal events, work meetings), current weather conditions, style preferences, and the availability of clothes (e.g., laundry status). The app can automatically filter out items that are not suitable for the weather—like heavy coats in summer—or items currently in the laundry, while still allowing weekly outfit planning that visually indicates clothing in laundry.

WearWise is designed to save time and reduce decision fatigue. For example, a busy professional or CEO can have the app generate an appropriate outfit for an important meeting without waking up early to plan, while teens and young adults can explore creative outfit combinations they wouldn’t normally think of, using only their existing wardrobe. The AI suggests complementary colors, layers, and accessories for the occasion and environment, helping users look polished while reducing unnecessary clothing purchases.

All wardrobe data is stored locally on the device, ensuring privacy and control. WearWise empowers users to make informed, personalized decisions about their style, streamline daily routines, and maximize the use of their existing clothing while building confidence and reducing stress over what to wear.
